About:

I’m a freelance journalist based in Washington, D.C and alumnus of the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism. Over the past half-decade, I’ve covered topics of all sorts in both print and radio — from travel to housing and gentrification, to high school sports and, most recently, energy and public utilities. I also specialize in reporting with data and public records. My work has been featured in a variety of publications, not limited to, but including: the San Francisco Chronicle, the East Bay Express, Oakland Magazine, KQED and others.
