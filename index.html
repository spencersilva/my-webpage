SPENCER SILVA: JOURNALIST

About

Spencer Silva is a researcher at Media Matters. His writing and reporting have appeared in the San Francisco Chronicle, the East Bay Express, Oakland Magazine, KQED-FM, and other outlets. He holds bachelor’s degrees in political science and Spanish from the University of Portland as well as a master’s in journalism from the University of California, Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism. His story “Laney Built” was included as a notable work in the 2020 edition of the Best American Sports Writing anthology.
