About:

I'm a freelance journalist and second-year student at the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism. Though most of my experience is in print, my focus at UC Berkeley has been radio production and data reporting. My first radio feature aired on the KQED's California Report Magazine in December 2017, and you can find my writing at the San Francisco Chronicle, Oakland Magazine and others.
